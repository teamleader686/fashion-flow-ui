## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I skip the profile completion?
Yes! You can click "I'll do this later" on the first step. However, you'll need to complete it before checkout.

### Can I change my information later?
Yes! Visit `/edit-profile` anytime to update your information.

### Why do you need my phone number?
We use it to send you order updates via WhatsApp and SMS. It's required for order processing.

### Is my birthday/anniversary required?
No, these are optional. But we recommend adding them to get special offers on your special days!

### Can I change my email?
Email is tied to your account and cannot be changed through the profile page. Contact support if you need to change it.

### What if I enter the wrong phone number?
You can update it anytime on the Edit Profile page.
